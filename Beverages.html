Caramel & Coconut Milk Shakes
Coffee creamer flavored like Girl Scouts® Samoas/Caramel deLites cookies makes this
milk shake taste divine! Chocolate, caramel and coconut combine in a delicious cookie-
tasting ice cream treat.

Yield: 2

Ingredients:

    2 cups vanilla or chocolate light ice cream
    1 cup refrigerated unsweetened coconut milk (not canned) or lowfat 1% milk
    2 tablespoons NESTLÉ® COFFEE-MATE® GIRL SCOUTS® Caramel & Coconut Liquid
    Coffee Creamer
    1 tablespoon caramel syrup or NESTLÉ LA LECHERA Dulce de Leche (squeezable bottle)    
    Toasted coconut flakes (optional)
    Chocolate shavings (optional)

Instructions:
    PLACE ice cream, milk and Coffee-mate in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.

   SWIRL caramel syrup around inside of 2 glasses. Divide milk shake between glasses.
                  Garnish milk shakes with coconut and chocolate.
